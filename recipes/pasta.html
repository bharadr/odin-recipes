Ground Beef Pasta Recipe

Ground Beef Pasta with Red Sauce and Linguini

This is a very basic, healthy recipe that shouldn't take more than 15-20 minutes to finish. It can be done by beginners and is ideal for someone who needs to meal prep on a regular basis.

Ingredients

  • 8oz lean ground beef (93% lean)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 85g of linguini pasta
  • 50g of diced onions
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup of chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup of Prego Marinara Sauce
  • 3 cups of water

Instructions

  1. For the pasta, have a small pot on the oven set to medium high. After 2 minutes fill the pot with 6 cups of water.
  2. Add all 85g of linguini pasta into the small pot, making sure as much of the pasta in submerged in the water.
  3. The pasta should finish cooking in 10 minutes, stir every 2 minutes.
  4. Place a large pot on the oven and turn the heat up to medium high.
  5. Immediately add the diced onions into the pot along with 1 tbsp of the olive oil.
  6. Add the tsp of salt to this mix and stir for 2 minutes until the onions become a bit yellow
  7. Now move the sauteed onions to the side of the pot and add the 8oz of ground beef. Use a spatula to chop the beef up into small segments
  8. To prevent this mix from drying up, pour 1/4 cup of chicken broth into the pot.
  9. For 4 minutes, let this mix cook, and then add the 1/2 cup of Prego Marinara Sauce into the mix. Stir thoroughly and let it cook for another 4 minutes, then turn off the heat.
  10. The pasta should be cooked now, so carefully take the small pot, drain the water off with a pasta strainer so that only the cooked pasta is left.
  11. Pour the cooked pasta into the large pot and mix thoroughly for 30 seconds to 1 minute with the sauce.
  12. The dish should now be ready to serve.
