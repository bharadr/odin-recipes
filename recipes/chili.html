Turkey Chili Recipe

Turkey Chili

This is a very basic, healthy recipe that shouldn't take more than 15-20 minutes to finish. It can be done by beginners and is ideal for someone who needs to meal prep on a regular basis.

Ingredients

  • 8oz lean ground turkey (93% lean)
  • 2 tbsp of Chugwater Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 can of black beans
  • 50g of diced onions
  • 50g of diced bell peppers
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup of chicken broth

Instructions

  1. Place a large pot on the oven and turn the heat up to medium high.
  2. Immediately add the diced onions and bell peppers into the pot along with 1 tbsp of the olive oil.
  3. Add the tsp of salt and pepper to this mix and stir for 2 minutes until the onions become a bit yellow
  4. Now move the sauteed veggies to the side of the pot and add the 8oz of ground turkey. Use a spatula to chop the turkey up into small segments
  5. To prevent this mix from drying up, pour 1/4 cup of chicken broth into the pot.
  6. For 4 minutes, let this mix cook, and then add 2 tbsp of Chugwater Chili powder into the mix. Stir thoroughly and let it cook for another 4 minutes
  7. Then with a can opener, open a can of black beans, drain the fluid from the beans (with a strainer) and pour the beans into the spot
  8. Let this mix stew for 2 minutes, and then turn off the heat. Scoop the dish into the bowl to eat the chili
